Hair Care Tips by Elizabeth

Moisturizing Your Hair

Keeping your hair hydrated is key to maintaining healthy, shiny strands. Use a deep conditioner weekly, focusing on the ends, and seal with coconut, argan, or jojoba oil.

Protecting Against Heat

Excessive heat styling can weaken hair. Apply a heat protectant spray before using tools and explore heatless styling methods like braiding for curls.

Regular Trimming

Trim every 6–8 weeks to remove split ends and promote stronger, fuller hair. It’s a reset button that keeps your style fresh and healthy.
